This is the color scheme for JASM Comp 3.
Created 09/03/20
Last updated 09/05/20


White:      External IO (user interface)
Light gray: Internal IO (JDI)
Light blue: ALU
Blue:       ALU registers
Yellow:     Control busses and registers
Orange:     Control wires
Red:        Micro-code
Lime green: Clock
Green:      Main busses
Magenta:    RAM
